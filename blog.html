The Genesis of Dev cycles



Dev cycles was founded in 1956 by the Munjal brothers in Ludhiana, Punjab, India. What began as a small bicycle manufacturing unit has grown into one of the largest and most respected bicycle manufacturers in the world. The company’s humble beginnings were marked by a focus on delivering quality and affordability, principles that continue to drive its success today. From its early days, Dev cycles has been synonymous with trust and reliability. Its commitment to producing high-quality bicycles has earned it a significant market share, not just in India but globally. The company’s vision has always been to make cycling accessible to everyone, fostering a healthier and more sustainable lifestyle.
